## top-p (nucleus sampling)
只要试得足够多，”自行车“还是有可能会被选中的 → LLM开始胡说八道: ”自行车的维修与保养“
<img src="./pics/截屏2026-05-23 下午11.45.24.png" alt="Alt Text" width="700">

概率低的词：`长尾词` (tail)，需要一个保安，把这些词都拦在门外：
- Top-p (Top Cumulative Probability 最高累加概率)
- 从第一名开始往下数，凑够P这么大的概率后，就关门，剩下的长尾词全部丢掉

e.g. top_p = 0.9 <br>
<img src="./pics/截屏2026-05-23 下午11.47.37.png" alt="Alt Text" width="700">

丢弃后，模型把头部选中的那些词(head) 的概率按比例重新放大，加起来重新 ＝ 100%，再进行数轴采样
- 保留下来的高概率集合叫 the nucleus(核),这也是 nucleus sampling 名字的由来

top_p = 0.6，只剩下两个词了 → 输出更稳定 <br>

## 总结
<img src="./pics/截屏2026-05-23 下午11.52.37.png" alt="Alt Text" width="800">

### temperature
The high level idea of how LLM generate answers is like, it will calculate the probability for all the words it knows based on a function called `Softmax`. The `temperature` is part of this function and it will affect the softmax in a way that:

When the temperature is low:
- the previous word with higher probability becomes even higher
- the previous word with lower probability becomes even lower <br>

Which makes the LLM selection even more stable and deterministic.

When the temperature is high:
- the previous word with higher probability becomes lower
- the previous word with lower probability becomes higher, and more likely to be selected

So if you want 
- more stable and deterministic response, set the T to lower.
- more creativity, set the T to higher.

### top-p
The high level idea of how LLM generate answers is like, it will calculate the probability for all the words it knows based on a function called `Softmax`. Some words may have very low probabilities (they are called the `tail words`), however, their chance of being selected is not 0. If they got selected, the LLM generation will not make sense. In order to prevent this, top-p will only look at those words with probabilities summation to the value it is set. 

e.g. 
- top-p = 0.9, it will only look at the words with a total probability of 90%
- top-p = 0.6, it will only look at the words with a total probability of 60%- in this case, even less words are retained. Making the LLM generation more stable.

### 什么时候调什么
通常默认就行 <br>
<img src="./pics/截屏2026-05-24 下午4.22.56.png" alt="Alt Text" width="600"> <br>
避免一个调高，一个调低- 它们会互相干扰: 你把 temperature 调得很高想增加多样性,但同时 top-p 设得很低(比如 0.5),那 top-p 会先把长尾砍掉,你拉高温度想"解放"的那些低概率词其实根本没机会被选中。两个旋钮在打架,你得到的结果既不是纯高温的效果,也不是纯低 top-p 的效果,
